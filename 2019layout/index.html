Who We Are

A space for all to feel welcome. Supportive, friendly, minigful international community encouraging diversity and inclusion in technology. The global PyLadies mentorship group has a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community with the mission to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings. PyLadies aims to provide a friendly support network for women and a bridge to the larger Python world.

Learning Together

Feel supported on our workshops and installation events. From begginers to advance! Our monthly meetups follow a short talk + workshop formula. You can find and join upcoming events on our Meetup group. Python novices and beginners are especially encouraged to join our next meetup. You can find the content of our talks, tutorials, excercises and workshops in our materials GitHub repository.
