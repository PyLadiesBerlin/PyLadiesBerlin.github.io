About

The global PyLadies mentorship group has a focus on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community with the mission to promote, educate and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events and social gatherings.

PyLadies aims to provide a friendly support network for women and a bridge to the larger Python world.
