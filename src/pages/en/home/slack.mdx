Most of the PyLadies chapters can be found in the PyLadies Gloal Slack, so in
case you want to connect, we recommend you to join!

We expect all participants in our events and community to abide by [this code of
conduct](https://pyladies.com/CodeOfConduct).

The following steps will help you join:

* Get an invite to our [Slack using your email
  here](https://slackin.pyladies.com/).
* Go to Slack in [pyladies.slack.com](https://pyladies.slack.com).
* Join channel `#city-berlin`, and in case you have interest `#germany` and `#jobs-europe`.
